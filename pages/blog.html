Facial Recognition - Abdullah Ahmad Haroun

Article: Facial recognition using facial features



Introduction and Background

Face recognition is in demand today due to its utility in person identification, where a small input image or a glimpse can help recognise the person from a database. This technology aids the identification or confirmation of an individuals identity using their face. This paper discusses the importance of face recognition in enhancing security and its usefulness in various fields. The challenges faced in implementing face recognition include different lighting conditions, poses, and limited training data.


Complete Overview of the Article

The authors analyze the effectiveness of two face recognition algorithms, facenet and facial landmarks, for crime detection, face verification, and human tracking. The project aims to secure industrial areas, identify individuals from crowds, track missing persons, and monitor anti-social elements. The proposed system uses a face recognition python module to encode features and match them with webcam frames. This technology provides a faster and safer way of identification, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic.


Conclusion

Facial recognition stands as a cutting-edge innovation, its significance in real-world scenarios cannot be overstated. This piece delves into how facial recognition technology transcends obstacles, enhancing security in industrial settings using image or video input. On a personal note, its potential is profound, particularly in high-security environments, as it promises heightened safety measures and enhances vigilance against societal threats.

S Saleem, J Shiney, BP Shan, VK Mishra
